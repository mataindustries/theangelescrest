AC100 Qualifiers: Fastest Legit Path

Short answer: Pick one official 50-mile-plus race with published results, train to finish steady, and submit your proof by the AC100 deadline.

Last updated: 2026-02-18

Checklist

  • Confirm current AC100 qualifier rules and submission deadlines.
  • Choose one realistic 50-mile-plus race with simple logistics.
  • Build a 16-24 week block focused on climbs, descents, and fueling.
  • Save your official results link and backup screenshot.

Steps

  1. Set your target AC100 year and count backward to qualifier timing.
  2. Pick the qualifier that best fits your life, not just your ideal race profile.
  3. Train for a controlled finish rather than an aggressive debut pace.
  4. Submit proof early so admin issues do not block your entry.

For race-by-race options, use the AC100 50-mile qualifier hub.
